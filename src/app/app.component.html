About Us

We the India Student Association is a hub to all the Indian students who have flown miles away from home to be a part of an elite campus at Texas Tech University. We as an organization, collaborate with every Indian student to make them feel at home. They all can look at us when they want to have some fun time, mini parties, technical talks, cultural fests and a lot more.

We are a family of over 2000 Indians always striving hard to mirror the Indian culture for newbies to Lubbock in search of home outside India. We help them revisit our Indian culture and live the heritage, the rituals and the festivities that they had to leave behind in their pursuit of knowledge. The ISA is an amalgamation of all people and cultures present across the entire Indian sub-continent, from the barren deserts of Rajasthan to the rainy mountains of Mawsynram and from the chilly white peaks of the Himalayas in Kashmir to the confluence of three water bodies in Kanyakumari. The ISA celebrates the unity in diversity in India where a Hindu stands in line to devour the mouth-watering Haleem during the month of Ramadan, Christians celebrating Holi and Diwali in full festivities and people of all faiths flock to a Sikh Gurudwara to pay their respects.
